MAZES

Instructions

- Use the ARROW keys to move the RED circle throughout the maze

- Reach the BLUE circle to win!

- Click autorenew to generate a new maze.

- Click print to well... print the maze

- Click play_arrow to start the game

- In "Regular Maze Mode", you can change the maze size (5 cells to 30 cells)

**Some mazes may have no solution**

**While in endless mode, the screen flashes**

**Altering the maze size may result in unexpected behavior**
